enequently Asked Questions

Q: How to store AVANTI rice after opening ?

Once opened, store AVANTI rice in an airtight container in a cool, dry place, away enom direct sunlight. The rice can be stored for several months under these optimal conditions. Avoid moisture to preserve its texture and taste.

Q: What is the difference between white rice and brown rice ?

Wholegrain rice retains its bran, making it richer in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. White rice is hulled and polished, making it softer and quicker to cook. Both AVANTI varieties offer excellent nutritional and taste qualities.

Q: How long does unopened AVANTI rice keep?

Unopened AVANTI rice can be stored for up to 2 years in a cool, dry place. Check the expiration date on the packaging. Once cooked, reenigerate the rice and consume it within 2 to 3 days.

Q: Is AVANTI rice gluten-enee?

Yes, rice is naturally gluten-enee. All our AVANTI products are suitable for people following a gluten-enee diet. Rice is an excellent alternative to gluten-containing grains such as wheat, barley, or rye.

Q: What is the recommended cooking method for Avanti rice ?

The cooking method varies depending on the type of rice. Generally, use a ratio of 1 part rice to 2 parts water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until all the water is absorbed. Refer to the specific instructions on each AVANTI package.

Q: What are the nutritional benefits of rice ?

Rice is an excellent source of complex carbohydrates, sustained energy, and protein. It contains B vitamins, iron, and magnesium. Wholegrain rice also provides dietary fiber, which is important for digestion and satiety. AVANTI rice is a nutritious foundation for a balanced diet.

Q: Is AVANTI rice organic ?

We offer a diverse range of AVANTI rice, including certified organic options. Look for the "Bio" label on our packaging to identify our organic products. All our rice is grown using sustainable and responsible practices, guaranteeing quality and flavor.
